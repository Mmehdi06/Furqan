Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Introduction

Furqan is a Quran reading app designed to provide an authentic mushaf experience on your iPhone. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about how the app handles your data.

In short: Furqan does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Furqan does not collect any personal information. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising frameworks, or any third-party services that gather data. The app does not require an account or internet connection to function.

Local Storage

Furqan stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your reading position (last page visited)
  • Bookmarked verses
  • Your preferred reading theme
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any server, cloud service, or third party. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

Furqan does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, crash reporters, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Furqan does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
